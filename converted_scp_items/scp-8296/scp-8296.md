**Special Containment Procedures** : Instances of SCP-8296-A employed by the Foundation are to be restrained and kept away from open air, then treated with Class-C amnestics and discharged. Otherwise, individual cover stories are to be drafted and provided for each instance of SCP-8296-A following their departure from consensus reality.
Due to the low frequency of SCP-8296 events,[1](javascript:;) further research and long-term containment efforts have been deemed a misuse of Foundation resources. Further proposals will be denied.
**Description** : SCP-8296 is a phenomenon which compels individuals, hereafter designated SCP-8296-A, to ascend to a high altitude during a thunderstorm before instantly erasing them from reality. In 59% of cases, SCP-8296-A instances are employees of the Foundation.
SCP-8296-A instances become unnaturally calm and content, expressing no desire other than to go outside. If prevented from doing so, they will become cold and still. These effects will appear to wear off at the storm's end, but will reemerge as soon as another begins. This can only be prevented by ending the instance's employment at the organization, and erasing all memories of the Veiled world.
SCP-8296-A instances unaware of the Veil cannot be saved.
**Addendum 8296-1: Interview Log**
**Interviewer:** Dr. Irene Dekker (O5-5)
**Interviewee:** Dr. Vanessa Miroz (SCP-8296-A-25)
* * *
**Dr. Irene Dekker:** Please state your name and position for the record.
**Vanessa Miroz:** Let me see the sky.
**Dr. Dekker:** Name and position within the Foundation.
**Miroz:** I've never worked for the Foundation.
**Dr. Dekker:** Oh? Interesting.
_< Irene jots something down in her notepad.>_
**Dr. Dekker:** What’s your CRV?
**Miroz:** I don't know what that means.
**Dr. Dekker:** Cognitive Resistance Value. You took a test when you were hired, Vanessa.
**Miroz:** I told you, I don’t work for the Foundation, and never have.
_< Irene writes down a few more notes. She takes out a set of colorful cards.>_
**Dr. Dekker:** These all have minor memetic effects. I'm going to show each of them to you, and you'll describe how you feel, okay?
**Miroz:** Irene, let me see the sky.
**Dr. Dekker:** I'm afraid I can't do that. Now–
**Miroz:** Yes, you are afraid. There is no need.
**Dr. Dekker:** That's not what I—
_< This is not an interview room. This is a living room, comfortable couches set before a dark television screen. A glass cabinet packed with ceramic oddities stands between two shelves, sweet potato vines growing from clear jars. There are pictures and paintings on the walls, but it is too dark to make out the details, just empty silhouettes. The apartment is not too far from her workplace, the Site visible from the window on a clear day. It is not a clear day.>_
_< She sits in front of her wife. She is still O5-5.>_
**Irene:** What—
**Vanessa:** Hush, listen to the thunder.
_< The room brightens. The sky roars.>_
_< Irene takes a deep breath, shaking her head. When she speaks, her voice is frail and shaking.>_
**Irene:** Dr— Miss Miroz, this is an interview. Answer the question.
**Vanessa:** We're home, Irene. It's okay.
_< Vanessa takes Irene's hand and holds it. The other woman looks around the room, tracing the familiar lines between the floor tiles. She looks back at her wife.>_
**Irene:** Vanessa, please. I don't understand.
**Vanessa:** You will. Look outside the window. There's no need to worry.
_< Irene glances to the side. Sheets of rain pour beyond the glass, twisting and whirling in the wind. There is a flash as everything goes white.>_
**Vanessa:** You will not understand from this distance. We must get closer.
**Irene:** I can't— procedure— you're being compelled, Vanessa, this isn't you! I can't let you go near the window. Please.
_< Irene stands up from the couch, then stumbles.>_
`_< Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8296 must be contained.>_`
**Vanessa:** It's okay, love. It's okay. I'm still me, and you're still you. You have nothing to fear.
**Irene:** No! It'll take you! I won't let it!
**Vanessa:** I'm not going anywhere without you.
_< Irene falls to her knees, sobbing uncontrollably. Vanessa crouches down and embraces her.>_
`_< SCP-8296 is contained within the sky.>_`
**Vanessa:** Deep breaths now. Tell me five things you can see.
**Irene:** Your gray eyes. The couch. The big window, the walls…
_< Lightning flares beyond the window. Irene breathes sharply.>_
**Irene:** A thunderbolt.

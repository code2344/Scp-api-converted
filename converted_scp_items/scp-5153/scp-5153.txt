Containment of Threat:

 In preparation for an imminent extinction-level asteroid impact event, 

ASCI-2000

Description of Threat:

 ASCI-5153 is a large asteroid that is presently on course to impact Earth in late June 1908 (N.S.). The effects of such an impact are expected to destroy all life on Earth.

Special Containment Procedures:

 SCP-6612 is housed in its original stable within Site-73’s Secure Outdoor Testing Facility. SCP-6612 is to be cleaned and fed daily.

Description:

 SCP-6612 is an adult red roan horse capable of human speech. How SCP-6612 does so is currently unknown.

Research is ongoing.

Addendum:

Initial Interview Log

Interview Log

[BEGIN LOG]

Researcher Agnew:

 Hello, SCP-6612.

SCP-6612:

 Hi.

Researcher Agnew:

 So, you can talk?

SCP-6612:

 You heard it straight from the horse’s mouth.

Silence for ten seconds

SCP-6612:

 This was a mistake.

[END LOG]

Addendum:

 SCP-6612 has not spoken again since its initial interview, and has displayed no further attempts to do so.

SCP-6612 has been re-classified as Neutralized, and remains in stable condition at Site-73.

**Addendum 8581-3 — Excerpt from Mx. Masterson's guest speech during Vanguard's introductory press conference on 'pataphysics:**
_(Mx. Masterson walks into the conference space, accompanied by Doctor Gore. The crowd immediately breaks into a chorus of murmurs, which ceases once Mx. Masterson raises their hand and grabs onto the microphone.)_
**Masterson:** Thank you all for coming.
Before I go into what I was brought here for, I'd like to go off script for a moment and make a few statements.
_(They pause for a moment, then turn to Doctor Gore, who gives them a nod of encouragement.)_
**Masterson:** _(Clears their throat)_ Okay, so, back in the mid-to-late oughts, I was a member of the artist collective Are We Cool Yet?.
I shouldn't have to tell you this, but AWCY? were not nice people. They didn't care about whether their installations killed, maimed, or mentally scarred their audiences.
But I didn't know that, back then. I was young and wanted to express myself. So I put together a short film, created a fake TV and patch of roses, placed them in a field in Hornor's Crossing, and set the film to play on a loop.
The message of the film I made isn't important. What's important was its effect. It drew people in, engrossing them, and then after a point made them vanish from this reality completely.
_(Mx. Masterson lets out a sniffle, tears welling up in their eyes.)_
**Masterson:** B-But I fucked up. I didn't consider that they might not have a way to… come b-back.
Toni, Stich, Brock, Devon, C-Clem… they were gone, and I couldn't fix it.
But I tried to.
I watched my own film, desperate to wrench my friends from whatever had a hold of them. And I found…
Nothing.
And then that _Nothing_ spat me back the hell out.
I was a mess, for a long time. If it wasn't for F— [a friend](https://scp-wiki.wikidot.com/the-not-too-distant-future) who helped me through my derealization and panic, I wouldn't be standing here today.
I left AWCY?, needless to say. I found a [new group of friends](https://scp-wiki.wikidot.com/gamers-against-weed-hub), ones who wouldn't judge me for my past, who were lead by someone who went through [circumstances](https://scp-wiki.wikidot.com/scp-1168) similar to mine.
The pain of what I've done has never truly gone away. I still feel that _guilt_ , gnawing at me from the inside. And that "Truther" helped open the wounds I bared for everyone to see, made me feel like I was about to die all over again.
_(They sigh, taking a drink of water. Doctor Gore places a hand on their shoulder in reassurance.)_
**Masterson:** I can't bring back the people I've lost. But I can atone in other ways.
I've tried talking with the surviving families of those five viewers, tried bringing them closure. If I was lucky, I would just get the door slammed in my face. Only one of them was gracious to let in, and let me use one of their names for what I have planned.
That's why I've decided to announce, with the cooperation of Vanguard, the Clementia Schweiger Recompense Fund. Used to help provide reparations to those who have lost friends and family to Are We Cool Yet?'s installations, mine included. I, of course, will be making a substantial donation with my own funds to help kickstart the proceedings.
I hope you and I can use this as a next step to our healing, and make this world a better place.
_(The crowd applauds. Doctor Gore squeezes their shoulders, then gives them a kiss on the cheek, before stepping back.)_
**Masterson:** Thank you, everyone. Now, for what you actually came for.
Tell me, what do you all know about _metafiction_?
